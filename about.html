Our Story

Founded in 2018, Prisha Interior Solutions began with a vision to create thoughtful, functional, and beautiful spaces that enhance people's lives. What started as a small studio has grown into a full-service interior design firm with a reputation for excellence.

Our founder, Prakash Datir, brings over 15 years of experience in the design industry, combining his passion for aesthetics with practical solutions that meet our clients' needs.

Our Mission

At Prisha Interior Solutions, our mission is to create spaces that inspire, function beautifully, and reflect the unique personalities of our clients. We believe in sustainable design practices and thoughtful solutions that stand the test of time.

We strive to make the design process enjoyable and stress-free, guiding our clients through each step with expertise and care.
